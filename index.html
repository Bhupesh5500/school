About Shreeyantra College

Shreeyantra College of Science and Technology is a premier educational institution located in Padajugi, Bharatpur-11, Chitwan, Nepal. Established with a vision to provide quality education in the fields of Computer Science, Information Technology, and Business Management.

  • Affiliated with Tribhuvan University for academic excellence.
  • Modern curriculum designed for industry relevance and practical learning.
  • Experienced faculty with expertise in their respective fields.

Our college offers comprehensive programs including BSc CSIT (Computer Science and Information Technology), BIM (Bachelor of Information Management), and BBM (Bachelor of Business Management). We focus on developing well-rounded professionals equipped with both theoretical knowledge and practical skills needed for the modern workforce. Our state-of-the-art facilities and industry partnerships ensure students receive the best possible education.

Why Choose Shreeyantra College for Your Education

At Shreeyantra College, we believe in providing an educational experience that goes beyond traditional classroom learning. Our comprehensive approach ensures students are well-prepared for their future careers.

01 What programs does Shreeyantra College offer?

We offer three main programs: BSc CSIT (Computer Science and Information Technology), BIM (Bachelor of Information Management), and BBM (Bachelor of Business Management). All programs are affiliated with Tribhuvan University and designed to meet industry standards.

02 What facilities are available for students?

Our campus features modern computer labs, a well-stocked library, comfortable classrooms, sports facilities, and a cafeteria. We also provide high-speed internet access and the latest software tools for practical learning.

03 How does the college support career development?

We offer career counseling, internship opportunities, industry visits, and placement assistance. Our faculty maintains strong connections with the industry to help students secure promising career opportunities after graduation.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about admission, programs, facilities, and student life at Shreeyantra College

What are the admission requirements for BSc CSIT?

For BSc CSIT, you need to have completed 10+2 or equivalent with Mathematics as a compulsory subject. A minimum of 45% aggregate marks is required. The program is open to students from any stream who meet the eligibility criteria.

Does the college provide hostel facilities?

Yes, we provide comfortable hostel accommodation for both male and female students. Our hostels are located near the campus with modern amenities, 24/7 security, and healthy meals. Separate arrangements are made for international students.

What are the placement opportunities after graduation?

Our college has excellent placement records with students getting placed in top IT companies, banks, and multinational corporations. We have a dedicated placement cell that organizes campus recruitment drives and provides career counseling to students.

Are there any scholarship programs available?

Yes, we offer various scholarship programs for meritorious students, economically disadvantaged students, and those from rural areas. We also have special scholarships for female students pursuing IT education. Contact our admission office for detailed information.

What are the class timings and schedule?

Regular classes are held from 6:00 AM to 2:00 PM. We also offer evening classes for working professionals. The schedule includes theory classes, practical sessions, and project work. Weekend classes are available for certain programs.
